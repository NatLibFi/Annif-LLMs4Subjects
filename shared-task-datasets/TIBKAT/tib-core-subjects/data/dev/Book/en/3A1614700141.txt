Undocumented: the architecture of migrant detention
"Architectural practice requires the integration of often complex technical construction requirements with an equal measure of empathy for the occupants of the spaces we envision. In the deceptively simple pen-and-ink drawings and tiny photographs in 'Undocumented: the Architecture of Migrant Detection' we find technical information and human empathy in tension, an inevitable outcome whereever architecture is called upon to oppress living beings."--
