Handbook of research on advancements in supercritical fluids applications for sustainable energy systems / Lin Chen (Chinese Academy of Sciences, China) ; Volume 1
"This book deals with energy conversion and fluid dynamics categories. It discusses supercritical fluids, from the perspective of a new set of fluid manipulation and operations in a wide range of energy related systems/applications"--
