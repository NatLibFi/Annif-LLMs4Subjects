Costs of Inaction on Key Environmental Challenges
Countries today face numerous environmental policy challenges, such as climate change, air and water pollution, natural-resource management, natural disasters and environment-related hazards. The costs of not responding to them can be considerable, in some cases representing a significant drag on OECD economies.&nbsp; This report provides introductory perspectives on the costs of inaction and discusses some of the future problems likely to be encountered in this very complex area
