Impact tectonics
This volume is the 8th in a series of impact books resulting from the activities of the scientific programme "Response of the Earth System to Impact Processes" (IMPACT), by the European Science Foundation. The papers were presented at the international meeting at Mora, Sweden, which was held as part of the IMPACT programme. The papers cover various structural geologic, geochemical and geophysical topics on research of asteroid impact structures on earth and mars
