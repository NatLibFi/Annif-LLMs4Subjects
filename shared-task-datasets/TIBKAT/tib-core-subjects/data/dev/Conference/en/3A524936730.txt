Planetary Nebulae Beyond the Milky Way : Proceedings of the ESO Workshop held at Garching, Germany, 19-21 May, 2004
Planetary nebulae (PNe) are found in galaxies of all types and also between the galaxies in nearby galaxy clusters. This book contains the proceedings of a workshop held at ESO headquarters in Garching in 2004, devoted entirely to Extra-galactic Planetary Nebulae
