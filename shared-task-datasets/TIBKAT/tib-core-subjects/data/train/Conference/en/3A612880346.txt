Ninth ACI International Conference on Superplasticizers and Other Chemical Admixtures : [Seville, Spain, October 2009]
"In October 2009, the Committee for the Organization of International Conferences (formerly CANMET/ACI International Conferences) sponsored the Ninth ACI International Conference in Seville, Spain.  More than 50 papers from more than 20 countries were received and peer reviewed and 35 were accepted for publication in the proceedings of the conference"--p. iv
