Proceedings of the 35th IMAC, a Conference and Exposition on Structural Dynamics 2017 / SEM ; Volume 10: Topics in modal analysis & testing
"The collection presents early findings and case studies on fundamental and applied aspects of Modal Analysis, including papers on: Operational modal & modal analysis applications - Experimental techniques - Modal analysis, measurements & parameter estimation - Modal vectors & modeling - Basics of modal analysis - Additive manufacturing & modal testing of printed parts."--
