Emilio Ambasz: Casa de Retiro Espiritual : published on the occasion of the exhibiton of the Casa de Retiro Espiritual at the Museum of Modern Art, New York
Interactive CD provides a video clip (4 min., 12 sec.) in QT or MPEG-1 formats, which highlights aerial views and provides a virtual tour of the Casa de Retiro Espiritual including interior and exterior views, accompanied by music.  Also included are photographs of the structure and links to biographical information of Emilio Ambasz
