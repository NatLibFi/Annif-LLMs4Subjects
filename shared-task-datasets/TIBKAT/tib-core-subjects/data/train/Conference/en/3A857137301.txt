Proceedings of the 2015 Annual Conference on Experimental and Applied Mechanics / SEM ; Volume 7: Mechanics of composite and multi-functional materials
"The collection presents early findings and case studies on a wide range of areas, including: Multifunctional materials - Hybrid materials - Novel composites - Nano- and particle-reinforced composites - Additive manufacturing of composites - Digital imaging of composites - Damage detection - Non-destructive evaluation - Fatigue and fracture of composites - Manufacturing and joining of composites - Advanced composites applications."--
