Environmental Forensics : proceedings of the 2009 INEF Annual Conference ; [proceedings of Advances in Environmental Forensics held at Calgary, Canada in August 2009]
This publication includes peer-reviewed manuscripts from the 2009 International Network of Environmental Forensics (INEF) held in Calgary, Canada on August 31 through September 1, 2009. INEF is an organization founded by environmental forensic scientists for the express purpose of sharing and disseminating environmental forensic information to the international scientific community.
