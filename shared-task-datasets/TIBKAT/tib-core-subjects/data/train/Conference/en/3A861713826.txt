Dry Valley Drilling Project bulletin : DVDP / U.S. National Science Foundation, Office of Polar Programs ; 6: Dry Valley Drilling Project (DVDP) Seminar - II
"This Bulletin contains 29 contributions dealing with general programs or results of specific investigations relating to the Dry Valley Drilling Project. The core material investigated was obtained from boreholes drilled in McMurdo Sound, on Ross Island and in the Dry Valley area."
