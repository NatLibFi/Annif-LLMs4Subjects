New Trends in Fluid Mechanics Research : Proceedings of the Fifth International Conference on Fluid Mechanics (Shanghai, 2007)
Contains topics that include flow instability and turbulence, aerodynamics and gas dynamics, hydrodynamics, industrial and environmental fluid mechanics, biofluid mechanics, geophysical fluid mechanics, plasma and magneto-hydrodynamics, multiphase flows, non-Newtonian flows and flows in porous media, and flow of reacting fluid
