Max Krajewsky : Architekturfotograf zwischen Handwerk und Kunst ; [anlässlich der Ausstellung "Max Krajewsky - Chronist der Berliner Baugeschichte, Architektur- und Industriefotograf zwischen Handwerk und Kunst", Stiftung Deutsches Technikmuseum Berlin, 2. März bis 27. Juni 2010]
The architectural/industrial photographer Max Krajewsky (1892-1972) recorded with his camera the origins of numerous Berlin buildings for more than 50 years.  Four hundred and ninety photographs of the renowned photographer from the 1920s through the 1960s are preserved in the Historical Archive of the Stiftung Deutsches Technikmuseum Berlin. Presentation of his work presents a significant challenge, as neither negatives or glassplates have been retained, but only the original photos themselves
