Proceedings of the 34th IMAC, a Conference and Exposition on Structural Dynamics 2016 / SEM ; Volume 3: Model validation and uncertainty quantification
"The collection presents early findings and case studies on fundamental and applied aspects of Structural Dynamics, including papers on: Uncertainty quantification & model validation - Uncertainty propagation in structural dynamics - Bayesian & Markov Chain Monte Carlo methods - Practical applications of MVUQ - Advances in MVUQ & model updating - Robustness in desgin & validation - Verification & validation methods."--
