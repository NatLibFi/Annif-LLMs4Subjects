Proceedings of the 33rd IMAC, a Conference and Exposition on Structural Dynamics, 2015; [a Conference and Exposition on Balancing Simulation and Testing; held in Orlando, Florida, February 2-5, 2015] / [SEM, Society for Experimental Mechanics] ; Vol. 2: Dynamics of civil structures
"The collection presents early findings and case studies on fundamental and applied aspects of Structural Dynamics, including papers on: Model parameter identification - Dynamic testing of civil structures - Human induced vibrations of civil structures - Correlation & updating - Operational modal analysis - Damage detection of structures - Bridge structures - Damage detection models - Experimental techniques for civil structures."--
