Combined and hybrid adsorbents : fundamentals and applications ; [proceedings of the NATO Advanced Research Workshop on Combined and Hybrid Adsorbents: Fundamentals and Applications, Kiev, Ukraine, 15 - 17 September 2005]
Constitutes a review of the achievements in the synthesis, characterization and applications of hybrid organic-inorganic materials and of carbon and combined adsorbents. This work also addresses the modeling of the adsorption process, including the simulation of carbon masks used for both civil and military protection purposes
