Tenth ACI International Conference on Recent Advances in Concrete Technology and Sustainability Issues : [proceedings ; Seville, Spain, October 2009]
"In October 2009, the American Concrete Institute in association with several organizations in Canada, Europe and the United States, sponsored the Tenth ACI International Conference on Advances in Concrete Technology in Seville, Spain.  The proceedings of the conference consisting of 20 refereed papers were published as SP-261."--p. iv
