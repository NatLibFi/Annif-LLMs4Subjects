Parameterized and exact computation : first international workshop, IWPEC 2004, Bergen, Norway, September 14 - 17, 2004 ; proceedings
This book constitutes the refereed proceedings of the First International Workshop on Parameterized and Exact Computation, IWPEC 2004, held in Bergen, Norway, in September 2004. The 25 revised full papers presented together with an invited paper were carefully reviewed and selected from 47 submissions. The topics addressed&nbsp;focus on&nbsp;all current issues in this new approach to designing algorithms
