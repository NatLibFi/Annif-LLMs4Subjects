Generalized Lie theory in mathematics, physics and beyond ; [International Workshop of the Baltic-Nordic "Algebra, Geometry and mathematical Physics" Network, that took place at the Centre for Mathematical Sciences in Lund University, Lund Sweden on October 12 - 14, 2006]
The goal of this book is to extend the understanding of the fundamental role of generalizations of Lie theory and related non-commutative and non-associative structures in mathematics and physics. All the contributions have been refereed
