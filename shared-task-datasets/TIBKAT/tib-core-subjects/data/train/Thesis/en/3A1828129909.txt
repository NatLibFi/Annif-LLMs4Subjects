Rechtsfragen algorithmischer Preisdiskriminierung : eine rechtsgebietsübergreifende Untersuchung
Felix Rützel’s assessment of algorithmic price discrimination focuses on the connections between various areas of law. By outlining the existing legal frameworks for each and pinpointing where changes need to be made in order to react to this pricing strategy, the author’s analysis offers practical, interest-based solutions that close the gaps in current research.
