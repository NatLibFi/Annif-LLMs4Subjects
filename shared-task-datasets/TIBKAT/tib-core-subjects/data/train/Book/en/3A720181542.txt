Erfolgreich recherchieren - Erziehungswissenschaften
The volume Erfolgreich recherchieren - Erziehungswissenschaften (Successful Research Strategies: Educational Sciences) provides a comprehensive overview of information resources for the educational sciences. The book allows the student to quickly learn all the relevant essentials about library catalogs, specialized bibliographies, internet search engines, newspaper resources, and reference works.  n.a.
