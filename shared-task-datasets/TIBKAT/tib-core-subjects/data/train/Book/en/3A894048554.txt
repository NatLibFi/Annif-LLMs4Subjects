Matrix metalloproteinases and tissue remodeling in health and disease / edited by Raouf A. Khalil (Harvard Medical School, Brigham and Women's Hospital, Division of Vascular Surgery, Boston, MA, United States) ; Part 2: Target tissues and therapy
Matrix Metalloproteinases and Tissue Remodeling in Health and Disease: Target Tissues and Therapy, Volume 148, is the latest volume in the Progress in Molecular Biology and Translational Science series. This volume is the second part of a thematic on matrix metalloproteinases and tissue remodeling in health and disease, focusing on cardiovascular remodeling
