Arquitectos e ingenieros poblanos del siglo XX
"Biographic catalogue of the most representative architects and engineers that have worked in the city of Puebla. The book is divided in 5 chapters and presents detailed biographies of the architects and engineers active from 1879 to 2005. Documented with original official papers, plans and photographs of the buildings, before and after their construction, perspective drawings, lithographic material and an extensive bibliography"--Provided by vendor
