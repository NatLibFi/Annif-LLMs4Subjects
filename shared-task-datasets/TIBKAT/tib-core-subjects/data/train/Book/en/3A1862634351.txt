Hydrotreating and hydrocracking processes in refining technology
"An industry expert details the differences in hydrotreating and hydrocracking processes and the types of feedstock used for each. Relevant process data is provided, and process operations are fully described. This practical and accessible guide is written for managers, professionals and technicians as well as graduate students transitioning into the refining industry. Key Features: Describes feedstock evaluation and the effects of elemental, chemical and fractional composition. Details biodesulfurization, biodenitrogenation, and biodemetallization processes. Explores the process options and parameters involved in hydrotreating and hydrocracking. Considers next generation processes and developments"--
