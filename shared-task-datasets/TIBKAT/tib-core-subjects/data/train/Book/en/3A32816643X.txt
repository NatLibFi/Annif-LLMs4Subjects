Power generation : a selection of reports presenting research fidings from highly professional and skilled engineers, originally prepared under TWI's core research programme for industrial members
Contains the following reports: A review of techniques to manage penetration variability during TIG welding, P C J Anderson and M D F Harvey; An appraisal of methods for quantifying the performance of ultrasonic inspection techniques for detecting and sizing weld flaws, B W Kenzie; Real time defect assessment from ultrasonic test data, R M Andrews and A M Lank; Heat affected zone hydrogen cracking of C-Mn steels when welding with tubular cored wires, A J Kinsey.
