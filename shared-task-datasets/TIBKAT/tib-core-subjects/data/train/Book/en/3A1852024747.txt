Green technologies for waste management : a wealth from waste approach
"Proper waste disposal is a serious concern worldwide. This book addresses various types of wastes such as industrial, agricultural, and municipal solid and liquid wastes, their generations, and the status of waste management in developed and developing countries. It discusses advanced green technologies used in harnessing energy and bioproducts from wastes such as electricity, biofuel, biopolymers, fertilizers, and chemicals, without damaging the quality of the environment, but rather creating an alternate source of energy. Through many applications and case studies, this comprehensive book helps readers build a state-of-the art knowledge on waste utilization and energy generation"--
