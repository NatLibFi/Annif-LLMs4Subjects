The Everglades handbook : understanding the ecosystem
"This fifth edition is packed with scientific information about the ecosystem of the Everglades - taking into account how drastically the Everglades has changed. The book covers disciplines ranging from ecology, geology, climatology, hydrology, anthropology to conservation biology. The updated edition presents expanded treatment of subjects where our knowledge of the Everglades and its restoration has greatly improved. This includes a new chapter 'A Chemistry Primer for the Everglades' and discussion of the politics of Everglades Restoration. This text is essential reading for anybody trying to understand the challenges we face in restoring this unique ecosystem"--
