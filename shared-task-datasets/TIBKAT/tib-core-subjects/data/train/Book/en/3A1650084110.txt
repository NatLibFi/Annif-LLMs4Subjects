Modeling and Diagnostics of Polymer Electrolyte Fuel Cells
This volume of &quot;Modern Aspects in Electrochemistry&quot; covers the latest advances in diagnostics and modeling of polymer electrolyte fuel cells, presented by leading experts in the field. The volume is divided into two parts: part I reviews the state of the art in diagnostics of low temperature fuel cells, while part II covers the relevant modeling efforts
