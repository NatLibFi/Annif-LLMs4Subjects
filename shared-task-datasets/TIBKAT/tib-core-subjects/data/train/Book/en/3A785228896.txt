"The GNRation project for the old National  Guard's building (GNR) was the legacy of a headquarters program for the organization of Braga European Youth Capital 2012. We tested a strategy for low cost rehabilitation and a dynamic of uses that would strengthen the clustering of creative agents in Braga's urban centre, in an attempt to gather scattered initiatives."--
