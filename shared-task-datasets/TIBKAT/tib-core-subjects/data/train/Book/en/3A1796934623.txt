Nanosensors for futuristic smart and intelligent healthcare systems
"An outstanding feature of this book is a collection of state-of-the-art reviews written by leading researchers and scientists in the field of nanosensor-based technology applied to healthcare system including wearable devices, real-time monitoring, data analysis, and data transmissions. This book discusses the basics of smart nanosensors technology and their potential applications in healthcare systems. The book presents a comprehensive view of nanosensors and up-to-date communications systems and offering future perspectives on next-generation medical care systems. This book will be immensely useful to scientists, researchers, and technocrats in the field of smart and intelligent nanosensors technology applied in healthcare systems"--
