Casa modernista : a history of the Brazil modern house
"A comprehensive volume on modern residential architecture in Brazil featuring 40 houses [i.e. 65 houses, 1927-2010]. Architects whose work is featured include: Oscar Niemeyer, Paulo Mendes da Rocha, Affonso Eduardo Reidy ... This book will present a selection of the most stunning residential work of Brazil's modern architects to reveal the ongoing themes and individual interpretations of Brazilian Modernism."--
