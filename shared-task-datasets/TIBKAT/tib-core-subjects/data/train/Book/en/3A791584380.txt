Interior design affects the quality of inpatient stay. From the pediatrics unit to the nursing home for dementia patients, patient/"customer" satisfaction is at the heart of the business model for healing and nursing internationally, and is increasingly important in Germany as well. Using the patient room as an innovative springboard, this book offers suggestions and solution approaches for interior design in hospitals. --
