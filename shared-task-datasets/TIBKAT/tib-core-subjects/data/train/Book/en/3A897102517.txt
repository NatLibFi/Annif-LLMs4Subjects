Belgian architects and their houses = Belgische architecten en hun huis / Muriel Verbist, Diane Hendrikx ; photography: Diane Hendrikx, composition and production: Muriel Verbist, texts: Hadewijch Ceulemans ; 2
"Photographer Diane Hendriks and stylist Muriel Verbist visit sixteen of the best contemporary architects or architect couples at work in Belgium today. Each chapter provides a portrait of the architect(s) and a photo reportage on their private home. Ground plans give the reader an even better insight into the projects shown."--
