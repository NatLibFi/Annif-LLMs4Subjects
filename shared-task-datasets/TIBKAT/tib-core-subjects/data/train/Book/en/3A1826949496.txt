Airborne circularly polarized SAR : theory, system design, hardware implementation, and applications
"A comprehensive resource on airborne SAR systems, this book explains the theory, system design, hardware, software, and applications of airborne circularly polarized SAR in environmental monitoring. Readers learn how to build the hardware and software of circularly polarized SAR systems and how to generate point target responses and images using the RDA from raw signal data. The authors discuss applications and analyzing techniques using a circularly polarized SAR system, making it beneficial for professionals, researchers, academics, and graduate students in Electronic Engineering, Radar Systems, Aerospace Engineering, Signal Processing, Image Processing; Environmental Remote Sensing"--
