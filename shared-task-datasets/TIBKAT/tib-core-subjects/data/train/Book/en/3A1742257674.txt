For introductory courses in computer concepts, digital literacy, or computer literacy, often including instruction in Microsoft® Office. Using technology to teach technologyBest-selling  Technology in Action Complete uses practical content and hands-­on projects to engage students in computing tasks and skills they can apply at work, in class, or at home. Designed for today s technically savvy students, the text introduces difficult concepts at a basic level early on, then expands upon these skills as students build mastery. The  16th edition brings innovative content updates, including new coverage of ethics, artificial intelligence, and managing a digital lifestyle, as well as several new features encouraging critical thinking
