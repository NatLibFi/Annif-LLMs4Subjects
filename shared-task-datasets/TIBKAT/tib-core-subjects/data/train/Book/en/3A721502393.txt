The ninth volume of this series provides a comprehensive presentation of the subject areas of sensor-operated and automated therapy systems for the restoration of physiological organ functions. A focus is placed on the interdisciplinary design of extra and intra corporeal technological systems that interact with the body's physiological systems, working in conjunction with or even partially replacing them. --
