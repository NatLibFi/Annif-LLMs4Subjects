Color and space in architecture and interior design : commercial, corporate, dining, educational, public, residential, recreational
Colour in Space is a constellation of the most outstanding space design projects around the world, displaying various application of color in architecture and interior design. It covers projects of all kinds, including public space, commercial space, educational space, hospitality space and many more. Within this book, readers can find the hues and contrasts; the light and shade of colors used in space design, having a glance of how it colors up can liven up any interior.--
