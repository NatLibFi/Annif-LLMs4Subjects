Engineering research : design, methods, and publication
"Research is one of the most important elements in the technical world and one of the most interesting learning features of college and graduate education. There is always a gap between the desire to explore new things and the capability to do so for the younger generations. Most universities offer a research methods course to graduate students; and many colleges have undergraduate research programs. In addition, organizations and companies frequently need to do innovative work to develop or improve products and process"--
