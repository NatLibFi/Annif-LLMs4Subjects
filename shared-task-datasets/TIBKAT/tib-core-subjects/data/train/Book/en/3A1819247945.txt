Bioremediation of toxic metal(loid)s
"The present book focuses on various bioremediation strategies starting from conventional to the biotechnological strategies for bioremediation of toxic metalloids. This book offers a bird's eye view on various bioremediation technologies based on variety of biological agents viz. plants, bacteria, algae, fungi (such as yeasts) etc., used for environmental clean-up of toxic metal(loid)s. It will cater to the needs of researchers, students and industry involved in bioremediation of toxic metal(loid)s"--
