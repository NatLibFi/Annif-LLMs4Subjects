Portuguese contemporary houses
This book is an approachment to Portuguese contemporary Architecture, its best exponents and participants. Generations of well known architects and their visions, since 1920 up to the generation of 1980. It features Siza's Vieira's, Eduardo Souto de Moura's, Carrilho da Graca's,Valsassina's, Manuel Mateus', Bak Gordon's and other notable architects of Portuguese contemporary Architecture visions
