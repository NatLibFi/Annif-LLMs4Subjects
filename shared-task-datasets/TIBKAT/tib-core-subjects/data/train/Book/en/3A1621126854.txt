Sustainable value creation in the fine and speciality chemicals industry
"Provides a tool kit for chemists and chemical engineers to design and develop economically, environmentally and socially sustainable practices in the fine and specialty chemicals industry"
"By combining business, scientific and technical issues that need to be addressed in order to achieve sustainable growth in the fine and specialty chemicals industry, this new book provides a tool kit for chemists and chemical engineers to design and develop economically, environmentally and socially sustainable practices.  Written by an author with extensive experience of the specialty chemical business combined with practical experience in manufacturing, R&D, chemical consulting and market research, it includes a number of industrial case studies from major international chemical companies working in the fine and specialty chemicals sector"
