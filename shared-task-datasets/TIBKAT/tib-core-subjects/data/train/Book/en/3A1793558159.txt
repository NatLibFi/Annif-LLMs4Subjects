Deep learning in visual computing : explanations and examples
"This book provides a unique insight into the explosive area of deep machine learning and how the challenges in visual computing can be tackled through this novel method of machine learning. The book introduces the reader to the world of deep neural network architectures with easy to understand explanations. From face recognition to image classification for diagnosis of cancer, the book provides unique examples of solved problems in applied visual computing using deep learning. Interested and enthusiastic readers of modern machine learning methods will find this book easy to follow. They will find this book as a handy guide for designing and implementing their own projects in the field of visual computing"--
