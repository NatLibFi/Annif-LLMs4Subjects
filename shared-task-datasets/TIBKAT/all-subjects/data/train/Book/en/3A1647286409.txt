Shoulder Arthroplasty
A practical and richly illustrated step-by-step guide to successfully performing shoulder arthroplasty. The comprehensive text covers various topics, and also provides specific examples of complications and how to avoid and correct them.
This volume presents orthopaedic surgeons and residents with a guide to successful shoulder arthroplasty. An introductory chapter on surgical approaches and preoperative evaluation precedes in-depth examinations of cutting-edge techniques. Topics include total shoulder replacement, glenoid component preparation, and revision shoulder arthroplasty
