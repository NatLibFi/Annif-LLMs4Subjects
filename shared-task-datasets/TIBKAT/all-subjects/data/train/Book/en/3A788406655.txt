Informationsmanagement : Grundlagen, Aufgaben, Methoden
Information management is the operational area in organizations devoted to managing information and communication and includes all management activities that relate to information and communication. This textbook and management guide presents the basic principles and functions of information management along with suitable methods for supporting these functions.  Univ.-Prof. em. Dr. Lutz J.
