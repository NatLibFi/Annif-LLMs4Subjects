Designing your world
A new book full of inspiration by Wolterinck will be released on 1 December 2019 by Terra publishers. Titled 'Designing Your World', it is a beautiful book for on the coffee table, full of superb photos of our most stunning projects. The book, designed by Trapped in Suburbia, presents over twenty Wolterinck projects, from the interior design of an exclusive yacht to the styling of a villa in Greece, from the latest products designed by Wolterinck to the lay out and planting of enchanting gardens
