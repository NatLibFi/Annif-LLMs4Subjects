Giving bodies back to data : image makers, bricolage, and reinvention in magnetic resonance technology
"Giving Bodies Back to Data focuses on the entanglement between the history and practice of data visualization across disciplines and cultures, focusing on the invention of MRI"--
Center-Periphery in Data Visualization : Concepts and Methods -- Histories and Practices in MRI Early Development -- Inside the Laboratory : from Signal to Coils, from Images to Bodies -- Visualizing Uncertainty in MRI Reinvention -- Intermezzo Lives in the Grid -- Challenging the Neuro-realism Fallacy through the Arts -- Archives and Laboratory Ethnography : Giving Bodies Back to Data -- Bodily Socio-technical Imaginaries in the Age of Operational Images.
