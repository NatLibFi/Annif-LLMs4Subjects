Color Atlas of Chemical Peels
First atlas to cover latest advances in chemical peel techniques Fully illustrated to support step-by-step procedures Ideal reference tool for dermatologists focusing on cosmetology
Chemical peeling is a method attempted at restore a more youthful appearance and improving dermatologic defects. An atlas on chemical fields, this book represents the state-of-the art in this field. Also, each author provides his/her personal experience and an approach with different peeling techniques in acne, rosacea, and other methods
