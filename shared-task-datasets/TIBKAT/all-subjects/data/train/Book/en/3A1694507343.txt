Mr. Guilfoyle's honeymoon : the gardens of Europe & Great Britain
Explore the grand gardens & forests of Europe & Britain with esteemed landscape designer William Guilfoyle, as he did with his wife on their honeymoon. The Guilfoyles took their Grand Tour honeymoon in 1890, at the height of William's reputation as the architect of one of the world's great botanical masterpieces, Melbourne's Royal Botanic Gardens
