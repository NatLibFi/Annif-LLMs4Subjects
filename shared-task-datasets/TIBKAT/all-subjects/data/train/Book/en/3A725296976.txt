Approximating prudence : Aristotelian practical wisdom and economic models of choice
In a unique undertaking, Andrew Yuengert explores and describes the limits to the economic model of the human being.  He develops a careful accoun of human action and motivation known as a "background account" that is both non-mathematical and comprehensive.  Approximating Prudence provides an alternative account of human choice, to which economic models can be compared.
