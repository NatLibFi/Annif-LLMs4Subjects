Pediatric Orthopedics in Practice
To "train to be straight" is the original meaning of the term "orthopaedics," which was coined over 250 years ago by Nicolas Andry. But how can&nbsp; the "trainers" straighten the crooked bones of children? Should we simply let nature take its course in the growth and development of children?&nbsp; Or do we need splints, braces, corsets, or even surgical operations, for straightening what is crooked?
