Proyecto, obra, comunidad : arquitectura habitacional moderna en Santiago de Chile
The author recovers the importance of the modern residential architecture in Chile. In particular, for the validity and sustainability of the large neighborhood units built by corporations of housing in the middle of the 20th century in Chile, under the direction of the Corporación de la Vivienda (CORVI, Housing Corporation) and the construction companies of  EMPART (Caja de Previsión de Empleados Particulares)
