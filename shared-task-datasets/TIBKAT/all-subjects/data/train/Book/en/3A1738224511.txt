Make your city : de stad als casco : the city as a shell : NDSM-Werf Amsterdam : NDSM Shipyard Amsterdam
Documents the history of the NDSM shipyard hangar in Amsterdam, a self-organized, self-financed space for small-scale manufacturing and arts community. As one of the initiators of the redevelopment of the former NDSM shipyard, Eva de Klerk gives a personal account of organizational processes, successes and tribulations, and complex relations with authorities
