Blue architecture : water, design, and environmental futures
Hydraulic or hydro-logic? -- Aqueous mediums, urban architectures, anadromous beings -- Liquid shaped space -- In concentrate -- Reconstituting architectural horizons -- Redrawing waters -- Epilogue: Reflections in depths -- Glossary of terms for the water-conscious designer.
"Architect and scholar Brook Muller writes that when it comes to water, the architectural design community is working at a level of sophistication and innovation that is about a generation behind where they are with energy. Muller's new book seeks to address that lag through a close look at a series of cases, mostly in the American West"--
