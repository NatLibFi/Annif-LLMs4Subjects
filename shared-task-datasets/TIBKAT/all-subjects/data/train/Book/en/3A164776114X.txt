Percutaneous Tumor Ablation in Medical Radiology
Thermal ablation has become an integral part of oncology. This book encompasses the different technologies employed in thermal ablation, its indications and the results achieved in various clinical conditions. It also discusses the techniques of guiding the applicators to the target structures by use of different imaging tools
