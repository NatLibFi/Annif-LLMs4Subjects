Completing the Foundation for Lifelong Learning : An OECD Survey of Upper Secondary Schools
This report provides internationally comparable data from upper secondary schools. It sheds light on how these schools are managed and financed, on their approaches to and difficulties in securing qualified teachers, and their efforts to support the professional development of teachers.
