Knott's handbook for vegetable growers
"Vegetable farming in the US represents a 20-25 billion business, employing over 125,000  people. In the UK it represents a L2 billion industry, employing 40,000  people Increasing health awareness has led consumers to improve their eating habits, which has resulted in an increased demand in vegetable consumption. Over the five years to 2020, production of both fresh and processing vegetables has increased, with retail prices also rising. Global weather conditions continue to have an impact on industry operations, resulting in the need for technological advancements to meet consumer demand"--
