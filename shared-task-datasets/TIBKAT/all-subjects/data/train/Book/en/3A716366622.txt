Nanotubes and nanowires
Nanotubes and nanowires continue to be a major area of research, with thousands of articles published every year. Six years have passed since the first edition of this book was published and this revised edition brings the reader up to date with an overview of the recent literature. Fully revised an updated with 20% new content, this revised edition of the successful Nanotubes and Nanowires is ideal reading for graduates needing an introduction to the field of nanomaterials as well as professionals and researchers in academia and industry
