Research-based programming for interior design
"This title provides students with the tools to understand how quality information can be gathered, analyzed, and applied throughout every phase of the design process. From the programming phase through contract administration, the text explores gathering information from previously published data and original data from interviews, surveys, and direct observation, and illustrates how interior designers consult and coordinate with other specialized fields. Research Inspired Design: Programming and the Interior Design Process prepares students to approach the practice of interior design as a knowledge-based activity and learn how to utilize research to develop strategies for design solutions and client communication"--
