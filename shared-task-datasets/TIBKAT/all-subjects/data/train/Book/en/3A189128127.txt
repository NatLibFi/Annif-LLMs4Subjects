Finite-element modelling of unbounded media
Aus dem Inhalt: Displacement, velocity and acceleration unit-impulse response with dynamic stiffness and rational approximation. Forecasting method. Consistent infinitesimal Finite-Element cell method applied to bounded medium. Damping- solvent extraction for dynamic stiffness and interaction force. Fundamentals of damping-solvent extraction method. Doubly-asymptotic multi-directional transmitting boundary. Concept and numerical implementation of doubly-asymptotic multi-directional transmitting boundary. Accuracy and modelling procedure of doubly-asymptotic multi-directional transmitting boundary.
