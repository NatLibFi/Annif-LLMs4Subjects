Competitiveness and private sector development, Republic of Moldova 2011 : fostering SME development
This report reviews the micro, small and medium-sized business sector in the Republic of Moldova, as well as governmental policies related to small and medium-sized enterprises, particularly regarding financing and innovation.&nbsp
