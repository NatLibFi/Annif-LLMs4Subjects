Gender, mastery and slavery : from European to Atlantic world frontiers
Introduction : the problem of gender, mastery and slavery -- Gender, mastery, and maternalism : Christian, Muslim and Hebrew traditions -- Gender, mastery and frontier : Europe, North Africa and native America -- Gender, mastery and empire : white servitude in new worlds -- Gender, mastery and nation : race and slavery in the United States
"Bill Foster's clear survey of this lively field provides a critical overview of current scholarship. Taking a thematic and comparative approach to fit with course needs, Foster couples case studies from the Americas with comparative perspectives from the ancient and medieval worlds, the Pacific and the Christian-Muslim slave frontier"--Provided by publisher
