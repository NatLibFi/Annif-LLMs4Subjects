Fundamentals of optics : an introductory course
"This book presents an introduction to classical optics and is written primarily for students of physics, engineering, and visual science. It is organized into four chapters: geometrical optics, polarization, interference, and diffraction. Each chapter is divided into units that emphasize the basic concepts of optics, accompanied by numerous figures and the necessary mathematical content. This book invites readers to study the fundamentals of optics and find pleasure in learning about this fascinating natural science"--
