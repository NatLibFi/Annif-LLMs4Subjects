The psychology of moviegoing : choosing, viewing and being influenced by films
"Here we are looking at how psychological theory can help us understand a common behavior, going to the movies. We investigate what psychologists have discovered about how we choose a movie, how we process the sounds and images of movies, and how movies may influence our behaviors after we leave the theater or turn off the screen"--
Choosing a movie. Personality and movie choice -- Emotion and mood -- The social context of choosing and viewing movies -- Experiencing the movie. Sensation and perception -- Learning from the screen -- Cognitive psychology and understanding movies -- After the movie. Selling watches, changing hearts and minds -- Depictions of the mentally ill, therapists and therapy -- Epilogue.
