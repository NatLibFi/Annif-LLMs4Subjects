Hydrology and best practices for managing water resources in arid and semi-arid lands
"This book addresses the hydrological processes of ASALs, climate change and variability, land use changes, floods and droughts, agriculture, integrated water resource management and sustainable development in the ASAL areas of the world. It addresses different aspects of Hydrology, climate variability, and water resources management and application of GIS and Remote sensing in ASAL areas"--
