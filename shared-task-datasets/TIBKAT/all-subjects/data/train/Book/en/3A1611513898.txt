Designed for those who want to gain a practical knowledge of modern computational techniques for the numerical solution of linear algebra problems, Numerical Linear Algebra with Applications contains all the material necessary for a graduate course on numerical linear algebra and numerous applications to engineering and science used in industry. With a unified presentation of computation, basic algorithm analysis, and numerical methods to compute solutions, this book is ideal for solving real-world problems. It provides necessary mathematical background information for those who want to learn to solve linear algebra problems using MATLAB, and offers a thorough explanation of the issues and methods for practical computation, avoiding an extensive theorem-proof type of exposition for practical use.--
