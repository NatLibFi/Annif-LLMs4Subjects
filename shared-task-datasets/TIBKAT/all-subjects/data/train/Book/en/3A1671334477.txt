The angry child : what parents, schools, and society can do
"This book will explore the basis for this growing fury in children and what can be done about it. The reader will look through a systemic paradigm of how we may be able to stem a tide of rage that threatens to drown the society of our youth"--
The storm on the horizon: the elements of anger and youth -- Parenting the angry child -- It is no one's fault....Lack of responsibility & the rise of anger -- The revolution of conflict resolution -- The match...What fuels anger in children? -- A kinder, gentler school...How anger can be handled in schools.
