Laser scanning : an emerging technology in structural engineering
"This book provides an overview on the evolution of laser scanning technology and its noticeable impact in the structural engineering domain. It provides an up-to-date synthesis of the state-of-the-art of the technology for the reverse engineering of built constructions, including terrestrial, mobile, and different portable solutions, for laser scanning. This cutting-edge edited volume is a valuable resource for students, researchers and professional engineers with an interest in laser scanning and its applications in the structural engineering domain"--
