Arquitetos do Brasil
"Subsidized by nearly 50 engineering and architecture offices from all over Brazil, this book offers an overview of the Brazilian construction market, including commercial sites, government buildings, private homes - in the city, at the beach, and in the mountains -, schools, hospitals, apartment buildings, factories, embassies, and so forth, as well as city planning in various states of the country. Information on individual architects is also provided"--Handbook of Latin American Studies, v. 58
