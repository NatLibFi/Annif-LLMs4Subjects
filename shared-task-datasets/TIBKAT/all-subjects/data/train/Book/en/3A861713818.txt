Dry Valley Drilling Project bulletin : DVDP / U.S. National Science Foundation, Office of Polar Programs ; 5
"Bulletin no. 5 is a report on the drilling completed during the 1974-75 field season in Taylor and Wright Valleys. Five boreholes were completed bringing the total number of holes drilled to 14. Preliminary reports are given on Japanese activities, seismological observations, geochemical, hydrogeological, and micro-paleontological studies, the distribution of secondary minerals, grain size distribution of sediments, and seismic profiling."--
