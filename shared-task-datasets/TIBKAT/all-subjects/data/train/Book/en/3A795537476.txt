Columbus, Indiana : Midwestern modernist mecca
"Columbus contains more than fifty buildings by noted modern architects - including I.M. Pei, Cesar Pelli, Robert Venturi, Richard Meier, John Carl Warnecke, and Harry Weese - as well as public art works by other internationally renowned architects and artists. Panoramic photographer Thomas Schiff skillfully combines his love of photography and architecture in Columbus, Indiana, presenting exteriors and interiors by these celebrated architects and designers in a singular panoramic format."--
