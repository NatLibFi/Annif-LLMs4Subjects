Time will not dim : American Battle Monuments Commission : a century of service, 1923-2023
"A richly illustrated book that offers an overview of the American Battle Monuments Commission's hundred-year history, with special attention given to the diverse artworks at ABMC's 60+ sites. It features major American artists-Paul Manship, Malvina Hoffman, Leon Kroll-along with artists' sketches, architectural drawings, and archival photos" --
