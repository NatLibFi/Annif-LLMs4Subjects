Count Dracula goes to the movies : Stoker's novel adapted
"First published in 1897, Bram Stoker's Dracula has never been out of print. Yet most people are familiar with the title character from the movies. Count Dracula is one of the most-filmed literary characters in history. In its third edition, this study focuses on 18 adaptations of Dracula from 1922 to 2012"--
