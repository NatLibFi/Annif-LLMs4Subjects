Handbook of international bridge engineering
The handbook introduces a single reference source that explores the state of the art and state of the practice for bridge engineering. This book spans 26 countries, accenting areas in Asia, Europe, North America, South America, Africa, and the Middle East. It examines the use of different materials for each region, including stone, timber, concrete, steel, and composites. It presents various milestone and landmark bridges including girder, arch, truss, suspension, and cable-stayed.
