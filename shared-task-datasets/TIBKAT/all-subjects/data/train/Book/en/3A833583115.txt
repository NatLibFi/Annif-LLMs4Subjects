Plant transcription factors : evolutionary, structural, and functional aspects
The only publication providing a comprehensive compilation of plant transcription factor families and their complex roles in plant biology. This publication discusses plant transcription factors. By including important aspects and unifying themes to understanding transcription factors and roles of particular families in specific processes, this volume makes basic concepts accessible
General aspects of plant transcription factors -- Evolution and structure of defined plant transcription factor families -- Functional aspects of plant transcription factor action -- Modulation of plant transcription factor action
