MXene-based photocatalysts : fabrication and applications
"MXene-Based Photocatalysts: Fabrication and Application introduces readers to the fundamentals, preparation, microstructure characterization, and a variety of applications of MXene-based photocatalysts. This work serves as an invaluable guide to advanced students, industry professionals, professors, and researchers in materials science and engineering, studying photocatalysis, energy, and environmental applications"--
