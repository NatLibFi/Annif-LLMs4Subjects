Coronaviruses with Special Emphasis on First Insights Concerning SARS
This monograph provides comprehensive information on the newest insights into the SARS epidemic and the responsible infectious agent. Background information on Coronaviridae (coronavirus/torovirus) and coronavirus/torovirus-associated infections in humans and animals is also given
-SARS has received much attention and coverage by the media and has a high impact on the public making this a hot research topic for scientists. -
