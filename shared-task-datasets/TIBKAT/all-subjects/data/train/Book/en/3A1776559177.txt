Removal of pollutants from saline water : treatment technologies
"This book provides a comprehensive understanding of technologies that are currently adopted in the treatment of pollutants present in saline water systems. It presents information on the treatment technologies for saline water systems, including seawater, brackish water, oil produced water, and other industrial saline wastewaters. The treatment strategies are based on principles including physicochemical, biological, membrane, advanced oxidation, and more. This book discusses the merits, demerits, and commercialization aspects of technologies and techniques through various chapters contributed by relevant experts from different parts of the world"--
