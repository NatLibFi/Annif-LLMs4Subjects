Ich und das Ganze : Wechselwirkungen zwischen mir und meiner Umwelt
The book shows the different worlds that surround the individual and how the individual can influence the whole. Conflicts become apparent. In order to overcome them, prerequisites are necessary. Individual chapters are devoted to these. Examples of urgently needed reforms of universities, broadcasters and the healthcare system in Germany are discussed.
