Finanzmathematik : Lehrbuch mit Übungen
Featuring numerous practice exercises, this textbook clearly presents the fundamentals of financial mathematics. It covers various types of interest, the calculation of annuities, installment payment calculation, investment calculation, depreciation procedures, as well as effective interest rates. The new edition has been corrected and updated (and takes into account new law on depreciation)
