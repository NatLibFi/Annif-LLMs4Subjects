Warfare in Medieval Europe c.400-c.1453
"Warfare in Medieval Europe, now in its second edition, offers considerably more attention to the transition from the later Roman Empire to the early Middle Ages, the composition of the armies of the opponents of the West, and the experience of commanders and individual combatants on the battlefield. Written in an accessible and engaging way, Warfare in Medieval Europe is the ideal resource for all students of the history of medieval warfare"--
Sources: The writing of medieval military history -- Military topography -- Military organization of medieval Europe -- Military logistics -- Military technology -- Medieval combat -- Strategy -- Two Campaigns in Focus.
