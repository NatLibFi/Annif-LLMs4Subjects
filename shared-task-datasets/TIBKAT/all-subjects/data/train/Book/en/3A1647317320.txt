Doppler Ultrasound in Obstetrics and Gynecology
Covers the clinical applications of Doppler ultrasound for the obstetrician-gynecologist, including the advances in 3D and color Doppler and the various techniques in 4D fetal echocardiography. This book covers the principles of Doppler ultrasound; the use of Doppler for fetal examination; Doppler echocardiography of the fetal heart; and more
Expanded and updated edition highlighting current standards and breakthroughs in the technology of Doppler ultrasound Includes latest advances in 3D and color doppler and 4D fetal echocardiography Includes more than 500 illustrations, including more than 150 in color
