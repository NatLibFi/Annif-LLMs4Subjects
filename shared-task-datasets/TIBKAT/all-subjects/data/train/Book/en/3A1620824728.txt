9/11 memorial visions : innovative concepts from the 2003 World Trade Center Site memorial competition
"Reflecting the 9/11 World Trade Center destruction, the 2003 World Trade Center Memorial Design Competition was open to anyone, drawing 5,201 entries from 60 countries, all of which were posted online. This book provides a first ever review of the 5,201 entries and tells the personal stories of more than 170 designers"--
