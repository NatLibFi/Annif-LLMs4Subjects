50 years of combinatorics, graph theory, and computing
"50 Years of Combinatorics, Graph Theory, and Computing advances research in discrete mathematics by providing current research surveys, each written by experts in their subjects. The authors of the chapters highlight open questions. The sections of the book, into which chapters are grouped include: Combinatorics; Graph Theory; Combinatorial Matrix Theory; Designs, Geometry, Packing and Covering"--
