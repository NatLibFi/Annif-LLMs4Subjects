Wiki works : teaching web research and digital literacy in history and humanities classrooms
"Wiki Works in the History and Humanities Classroom shows how teachers and students--working together as learning partners--can use interactive wiki technologies to transform the teaching of history and humanities topics through web-based research and inquiry-based learning"--
From print to digital -- The making of a history wiki -- Developing digital literacies -- Uncovering hidden histories and untold stories -- Exploring dramatic events and special topics -- Meeting people through biographies and books -- Engaging students using flipped learning
