Soil fumigation
"The second edition of Soil Fumigation is intended for individuals who plan to take an examination needed to gain either a Fumigation Qualified Applicator License or a Fumigation Qualified Applicator Certificate in California. The book covers basic principles of pesticide use, pest control with soil fumigation, and related laws and regulations. In addition, the book discusses the proper handling and application of soil fumigants, the human health risks and environmental risks of soil fumigants, and methods used to avoid or mitigate risks associated with soil fumigants"--
