Fundamental elements of applied superconductivity in electrical engineering
"Introduces the application of superconductor in electrical power technology and describes the general basics of superconductivity, introducing the experimental measurements of the parameters such as critical current, critical temperature, and critical magnetic field. Featuring discussion on the fabricating of technologies of practical superconducting materials, the author presents the structure of superconducting equipments and presents applications and case studies of superconductivity within the power system"--From publisher's website
