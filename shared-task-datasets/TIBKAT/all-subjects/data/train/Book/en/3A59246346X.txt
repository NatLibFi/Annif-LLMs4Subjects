The reification of desire : toward a queer Marxism
Disciplined bodies: Lukács, Foucault, and the reification of desire -- Performative masculinity: Judith Butler and Hemingway's labor without capital -- Reification as liberation: theory, practice, and Marcuse -- Closing a heterosexual frontier: midnight cowboy as national allegory -- Notes on a queer horizon: David Wojnarowicz and the violence of neoliberalism
Floyd brings queer critique to bear on the Marxian categories of reification and totality and considers the dialectic that frames the work of Georg Lukás, Herbert Marcuse and Frederic Jameson
