Imaging of the Sternocostoclavicular Region
Presents a review of the normal histopathologic anatomy, imaging techniques and diagnostic findings for a broad spectrum of clinical problems. This book describes the imaging of the anterior chest wall disorders. It includes a description of the different anterior chest wall syndromes and a logical classification of the disorders
Review of the normal histopathological anatomy, imaging techniques and diagnostic findings for a broad spectrum of clinical problems, including a classification of inflammatory syndromes previously described under several different terms.
