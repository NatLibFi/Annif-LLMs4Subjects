Design sprint : a practical guidebook for creating great digital products
"With more than 500 new apps entering the market every day, what does it take to build a successful digital product? You can greatly reduce your risk of failure with design sprints, a process that enables your team to prototype and test a digital product idea within a week. This practical guide shows you exactly what a design sprint involves and how you can incorporate the process into your organization. Design sprints not only let you test digital product ideas before you pour too many resources into a project, they also help everyone get on board--whether they're team members, decision makers, or potential users. You'll know within days whether a particular product idea is worth pursuing"--Publisher's description. Design, Produktentwicklung, Methode, Evaluation, User Testing, Anwendung
