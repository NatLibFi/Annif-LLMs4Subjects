The Kennedy tapes : inside the White House during the Cuban missile crisis
Transcribed from actual tapes made at the Oval Office - and enhanced by the commentary of historians May and Zelikow - the dramatic events of the Cuban Missile Crisis unfold in the actual words of President John F. Kennedy and his top advisers. Now available in a new, concise edition, this book retains its gripping sense of history in the making. 20 photos.
