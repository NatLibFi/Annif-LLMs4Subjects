Samuel Pepys and his books : reading, newsgathering, and sociability, 1660-1703
'Multitude of books' : patterns of reading in Peyps's diary -- Books, education, and self-advancement -- Pepys and news networks in Restoration London -- Reading history in the Restoration -- 'Books of pleasure' : plays, romances, and novels -- Buying books in Restoration London -- Books, manuscripts, gifts : scholarly and international networks -- 'Notes from discourses touching relition' : religious and scientific enquiry -- Libraries and closets : the uses of a book collection
Uses Pepys's surviving papers to examine reading practices, book collecting, and the exchange of information in the late 17th century
