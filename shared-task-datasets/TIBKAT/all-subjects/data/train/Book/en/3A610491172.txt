American flying boats and amphibious aircraft : an illustrated history
"History of the amphibious aircraft designed and built in the United States. Three sections: 1912-1928, 1928-1945, and 1945-present, with historical overviews of each period. Aircraft types are listed in alphabetical order, with technical specifications, drawings and photographs. An appendix covers lesser known types and several design concepts that never achieved the flying stage"--Provided by publisher
