Teaching English with corpora : a resource book
"Teaching English with Corpora is an accessible and practical introduction to the ways in which online and offline corpora can be used in English language teaching. Featuring 70 chapters written by an international range of researchers and practitioners, this book: provides readers with clear, tested examples of corpus-based/driven lesson plans; contains activities relevant to English for general purposes, English for specific purposes and English for academic purposes; caters for the needs of English language teachers working with learners at different  proficient levels; features flexible teaching suggestions that can be explored as part of a lesson (30-minute teaching plan) or as a full lesson (90-minute teaching plan)"--
