High-temperature solid oxide fuel cells : fundamentals, design and applications
The technology used in solid oxide fuel cells is easily obtained and their efficiency can be up to 60 per cent. The only drawback is the very high temperatures at which they operate which can be at 1000 degrees Celsius. This volume explores how they work and their likely real-life applications.
The technology used in solid oxide fuel cells is easily obtained and efficiencies for such devices can be up to 60 per cent. Their only drawback is the very high temperatures at which they operate. This can be of the order of 1000 degrees Celsius. The growing interest in fuel cells as a sustainable source of energy is pulling with it the need for sources which provide comprehensive and practical information on specific types of fuel cell and their application. This volume explores how they work and their likely real-life applications.
