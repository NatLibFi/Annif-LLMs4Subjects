Advanced Peripheral Nerve Surgery and Minimal Invasive Spinal Surgery
The papers in this volume summarize information on the most recent and effective techniques for treating difficult functional problems and painful situations using minimally invasive spinal surgery techniques. The book presents spinal endoscopy both for diagnostic and treatment purposes, as well as microsurgical operations for spinal problems, intradiscal techniques for the treatment of disc degenerative pathology, and dynamic stabilization techniques.
