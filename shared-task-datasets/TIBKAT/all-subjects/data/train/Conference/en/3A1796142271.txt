Including the Highly Gifted and Creative Students – Current Ideas and Future Directions : Proceedings of the 11th International Conference on Mathematical Creativity and Giftedness (MCG 11). 22.08.2019 – 24.08.2019, Universität Hamburg, Germany
This volume contains the papers presented at the 11th international Conference on Mathematical Creativity and Giftedness (MCG 11) Including the Highly Gifted and Creative Students – Current Ideas and Future Directions, held from 22.08.2019 - 24.08.2019 in Hamburg. This digital Book is open acces.
