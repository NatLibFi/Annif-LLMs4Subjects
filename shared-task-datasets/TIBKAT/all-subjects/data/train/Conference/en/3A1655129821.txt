Algebraic Geometry : Proceedings of the Third Midwest Algebraic Geometry Conference held at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, USA, November 14–15, 1981
On the geometry of elliptic modular surfaces and representations of finite groups -- On the rationality of certain moduli spaces related to curves of genus 4 -- A construction of special space curves -- Springer fibres with non-ample normal bundles -- The invariants of liaison -- On étale coverings of the affine space -- On supersingular abelian varieties -- Platonic solids, Kleinian singularities, and Lie groups.
