People in control : human factors in control room design
The book has 3 parts and is divided into 18 chapters. The first part deals with human error, memory skills, vigilance, situation awareness, teamworking and training for control room tasks. The second part discusses humans and machines, task analysis, training and technology for teams, naturalistic analysis of control room activities, development of a railway ergonomics control assessment package and the third part deals with control room mock-up trials, alarm systems design, decision support in process control plants, train controllers, interface design and mental workload, power generation, human-centred design and integrated platform management system design
