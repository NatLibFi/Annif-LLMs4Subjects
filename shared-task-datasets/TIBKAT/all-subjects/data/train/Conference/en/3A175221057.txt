Dry Valley Drilling Project bulletin : DVDP / U.S. National Science Foundation, Office of Polar Programs ; 4: Dry Valley Drilling Project (DVDP) Seminar - I : held at the University Towers Hotel, Seattle, Washington, on May 29-31, 1974 : program with abstracts
"Participants from three nations (United States, Japan and New Zealand) active in the Dry Valley Drilling Project convened for a three-day meeting in Seattle, Washington to report their findings and plan future research. This volume contains a list of participants, a schedule of the meeting, and abstracts of 36 papers dealing with general programs or results of specific investigations in the fields of geophysics, limnology, petrology, and environmental impact."--
