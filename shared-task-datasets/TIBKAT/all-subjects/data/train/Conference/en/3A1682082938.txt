Proceedings of ARCH 2019 : 9th International Conference on Arch Bridges
The book contains proceedings presented at the 9th International Conference on Arch Bridges held in Porto, Portugal on October 2 to 4, 2019. It is addressed to scientists, designers, technicians, stakeholders and contractors, seeking for an up-to-date view of the recent advances in the area of arch bridges
Keynote lectures -- Heritage arch bridges -- Analytic and numerical studies of arch structures -- Experimental studies of arch structures -- Design and construction of arch bridges -- Rehabilitation, maintenance and condition assessment of arch bridges -- New and future trends in arch bridges
