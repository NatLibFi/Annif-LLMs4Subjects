Advances in legume science and practice : at the Jurys Inn Hotel, Glasgow on 21-22 March 2018
" As interest increases in sustainable agriculture, food security and healthy diets, the understanding that legumes play a key role in all these fields is also growing. This two-day conference aims to bring together individuals from broad spectrum of disciplines to explore how legume research and practice is advancing, and what parallels exist between specialties."--Conference website
