This book constitutes the refereed proceedings of the First International Workshop on Advanced Internet Services and Applications, AISA 2002, held in Seoul, Korea, in August 2002. The 28 revised full papers presented were carefully reviewed and selected from more than 70 submissions. Among the topics covered are QoS architecture, reliability, security, Web acceleration, reverse/proxy caching schemes, content delivery networks, distributed/fault-tolerant architectures, storage/back-up solutions, media and streaming servers, switching technology, and home networking
