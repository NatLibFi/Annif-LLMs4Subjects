35th Hemophilia Symposium : Hamburg 2004
Contains the contribution to the 35th Hemophilia Symposium, Hamburg 2004. This book also features papers and posters on hemophilia and hemorrhagic disorders and inhibitors in hemophilia. It covers epidemiology, risk of infections and inhibitors in hemophilia, chronic hemophilic synovitis and long-term results of orthopedic treatment, and more
