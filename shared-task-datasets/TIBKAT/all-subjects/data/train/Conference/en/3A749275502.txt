This book constitutes the refereed proceedings of the 14th Brazilian Symposium on Artificial Intelligence, SBIA'98, held in Porto Alegre, Brazil, in November 1998. The 26 revised full papers presented were carefully reviewed and selected from a total of 61 submissions. The papers are organized in topical sections on multi-agent systems, intelligent tutoring systems, natural language processing, machine learning and neural networks, logic programming, knowledge representation, AI applications
