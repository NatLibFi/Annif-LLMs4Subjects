Venetian glass by Carlo Scarpa: the Venini Company, 1932 - 1947 : [catalogue ... in conjunction with the Exhibition "Venetian Glass by Carlo Scarpa: The Venini Company, 1932-1947", on view at The Metropolitan Museum of Art, New York, from November 5, 2013, to March 2, 2014]
This volume reconstructs the fifteen years of the remarkable artistic collaboration between Carlo Scarpa and the Venini glass-making company, from 1932 to 1947
