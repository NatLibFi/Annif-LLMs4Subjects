Proceedings of the 33rd IMAC, a Conference and Exposition on Structural Dynamics, 2015; [a Conference and Exposition on Balancing Simulation and Testing; held in Orlando, Florida, February 2-5, 2015] / [SEM, Society for Experimental Mechanics] ; Vol. 3: Model validation and uncertainty quantification
