Precision Assembly Technologies and Systems : 5th IFIP WG 5.5 International Precision Assembly Seminar, IPAS 2010, Chamonix, France, February 14-17, 2010. Proceedings
This book constitutes the refereed proceedings of the 5th International Precision Assembly Seminar, IPAS 2010, in Chamonix, France, in February 2010. The 39 revised full papers were carefully reviewed and selected from numerous submissions. The papers are organized in four parts: design of micro products; micro-assembly processes; positioning and manipulation for micro-assembly; and development of micro-assembly production
