Der Sozialstaat in Deutschland und Europa : Berichte und Diskussionen auf der Tagung der Vereinigung der Deutschen Staatsrechtslehrer in Jena vom 6. bis 9. Oktober 2004
[Publications from the Association of German Constitutional Law Teachers, Vol. 63]The volume reproduces the reports and discussions at the conference of the Association of German Constitutional Law Teachers in Jena from 6th to 9th October 2004. ContentChristoph Enders / Ewald Wiederin, The nature of the social state in the field of conflict from self-responsibility and welfare; Rainer Pitschas / Helge Sodan, The future of social security systems; Peter J. Tettinger / Jens-Peter Schneider, Administrative law instruments of the social state; Matthias Jestaedt / Gabriele Britz , Discrimination protection and private autonomy
