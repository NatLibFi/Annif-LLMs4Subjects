Off-farm labor supply and various related aspects of resource allocation by agricultural households
This dissertation deals with off-farm labor supply of agricultural households in Germany in three different contexts of resource allocation; the farm labor supply behavior, the intrahousehold interdependence in time allocation, and the occupational choise in the context of dynamic agricultrual structural change.
