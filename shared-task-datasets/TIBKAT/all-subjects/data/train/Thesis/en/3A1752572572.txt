Theory of victimization of educated women in India : Understanding experts’ perception of a cognitive shift
In India, presently, gender-based violence is increasing at an alarming rate but at the same time due to state’s effort female literacy rate rising too. In the light of such scenario, the theory of victimization of educated women provides an overall analysis of causes of violence, impact of education on women empowerment and changing nature of gender-based violence in modern India. To make the thesis all-inclusive, the broader definition of education is being used. Here education includes informal education, which is analysed through the role of media.
