Plant nutrient mobilization and acquisition strategies : adaptation to water and nutrient availability
Distribution and variability of precipitation are predicted to shift in Chile and are expected to lead to increases in frequency and duration of droughts. These developments can lead to a cascade of environmental changes that are likely to alter biogeochemical cycles and ecosystem functioning. The ability of vegetation to adapt to changing conditions affects the response of natural ecosystems to predicted precipitation changes.
