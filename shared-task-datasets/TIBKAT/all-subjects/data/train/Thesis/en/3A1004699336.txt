Entity-centric text mining for historical documents
Recent years have seen an important increase of digitization projects in the cultural heritage domain. As a result, growing efforts have been directed towards the study of natural language processing technologies that support research in the humanities. This thesis is a contribution to the study and development of new text mining strategies that allow a better exploration of contemporary history collections from an entity-centric perspective.
