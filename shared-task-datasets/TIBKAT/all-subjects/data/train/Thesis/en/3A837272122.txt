Social media mining as an opportunistic citizen science model in ecological monitoring: a case study using invasive alien species in forest ecosystems.
Major environmental, social and economic changes threatening the resilience of ecosystems world-wide and new demands on a broad range of forest ecosystem services present new challenges for forest management and monitoring. New risks and threats such as invasive alien species imply fundamental challenges for traditional forest management strategies, which have been based on assumptions of permanent ecosystem stability.
