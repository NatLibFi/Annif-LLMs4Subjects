Feasibility assessment of waste management and treatment in Jordan
In this research, several approaches of waste management have been studied through experimental projects. They were selected to be the core of this research, and considered the critical obstacles for the waste management sector in Jordan. However, different areas in the field of waste management have been addressed, including: improving solid waste collection using ArcGIS technique; assessment of compost production (with quality concerns); examine RDF production from mixed MSW as a substitute fuel for the cement industry; proposing different waste treatment & disposal sustainable solutions.<eng>
