Theodor Nöldeke (1836-1930) was one of the most famous orientalists of the 19th century who dealt not only with Oriental languages, but addressed also historical questions. Nöldeke was thus called both a philologist and a historian. Among Nöldeke’s most important works is his Geschichte des Qorāns(“History of the Quran”)which was published in 1860. His Geschichte des Qorāns consists of three parts: 1) On the Origin of the Quran. This part was revised by Friedrich Schwally (1863-1919) and published in 1909. 2) The Collection of the Quran.
