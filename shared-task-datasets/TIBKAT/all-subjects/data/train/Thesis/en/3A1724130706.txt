Solid waste management in tourism destinations in Tunisia : diagnostic and improvement approaches
Tourism sector represents an important economic activity in Tunisia. The cleanliness of the tourism destinations is an important parameters to ensure the development of the sector. In this work, a key set of indicators were developed for solid waste management in tourism in Tunisia. Based on this, the situation was diagnosted, and sustainable organisational, financial and technical solutions were developed to ensure clean, circular and sustainable tourism throughout the year.<eng>
