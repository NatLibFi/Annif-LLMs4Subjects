Evolutionary innovations : the business of biotechnology
his is a study of the commercial development of biotechnology which compares the initiatives, activities, and organization of two firms--Genetech in the UIS and Kabi in Sweden as they brought knowledge to the market in the form of insulin and the human growth hormone. Writing from a broad evolutionary perspective, this important study of one of the most modern science-based technologies will be of interest to all concerned with understanding the processes of innovation.
