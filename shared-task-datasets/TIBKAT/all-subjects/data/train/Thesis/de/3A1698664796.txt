Aboveground biomass (AGB) is related to the carbon content of the forest and forest carbon is a core variable for contemporary forest management and policy decisions. A credible and precise estimate of AGB is a prerequisite for the scientifically proper implementation of commitments made regarding the reduction of forest carbon emissions.
