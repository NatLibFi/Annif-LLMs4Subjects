In Mexico there is a need for technologies that link forest stand dynamics with silviculture systems. Providing these technologies could enhance forest management, not only by making forests more profitable, but by adapting them to climate change. Lately, there has been a debate on forest management for carbon and climate change benefits. Recent studies claim that letting forest grow without management is better for carbon sequestration and storage.
