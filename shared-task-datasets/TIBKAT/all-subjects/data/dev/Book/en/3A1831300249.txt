Successful development of green building projects
"Focusing on office buildings, this explores how green building development can be managed to achieve successful project outcomes, allowing project decision-makers such as clients and project team members to consider identified success conditions. Highlighting 73 success conditions which have been categorized within 20 broad themes, the book reports on the findings from interviews with Green Building stakeholders from Australia, Hong Kong, Pakistan, Singapore, the United Arab Emirates, and the United Kingdom. Findings can inform future research for more efficient development of green building projects"--
