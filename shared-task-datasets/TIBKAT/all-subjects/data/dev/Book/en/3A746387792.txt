Focus on Apple Aperture : [focus on the fundamentals]
This straightforward, inspiring guide is aimed at photographers whose goal is to use the software to bring out the best in their photographs. Professional photographer and educator Corey Hilz leads you through a highly effective, professional's workflow with Aperture, from importing your photos from your camera and making image adjustments, to labeling each photo with metadata and keywords for effective organization and searching, and finally outputting your images for print or social media
