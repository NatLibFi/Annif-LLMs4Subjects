The relation between agricultural and non-agricultural economic development : technical report on an empirical analysis of European regions
Support of agriculture is justified, among others, by its contribution to rural economic development. Nevertheless, the relation between agricultural and general economic development may be ambiguous. On the one side, agriculture may affect other sectors positively via multiplier and income effects.
