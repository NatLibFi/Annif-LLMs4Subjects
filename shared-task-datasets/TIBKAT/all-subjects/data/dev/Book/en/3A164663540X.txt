Review of Surgery : Basic Science and Clinical Topics for ABSITE
The American Board of Surgery In-Training Examination (ABSITE) is taken annually by more than 7000 general surgery residents nationwide to ensure that specific learning objectives are being met in their training. Recent exams have emphasized basic science and Review of Surgery: Basic Science, Clinical Topics for ABSITE is written with that in mind. Each chapter is a short summary of a basic science or clinical topic and an accompanying CD-ROM includes hundreds of related questions like those found on ABSITE. The book/CD is targeted to trainees at various levels and also can be used to prepare for the qualifying (written) and certifying (oral) exams given by the American Board of Surgery upon completion of training.
