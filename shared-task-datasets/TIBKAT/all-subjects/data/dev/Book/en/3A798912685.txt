Soil solarization : theory and practice
"This book brings up to date a range of topics concerning the disinfestation of soils of plant pathogens and pests, innovations in the implementation of integrated pest management practices, and the importance of these technologies in view of changes in the kinds and uses of soil fumigants. It summarizes new postplant treatments for controlling plant pests and pathogens and the technology of soil disinfestation in various cropping systems. The main focus of this book is the solar heating of soils, now known as soil solarization, a procedure for soil disinfestation ..."--
