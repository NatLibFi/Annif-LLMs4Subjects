Image-Based Modeling
This book guides you in the journey of 3D modeling from the theory with elegant mathematics to applications with beautiful 3D model pictures. Written in a simple, straightforward, and concise manner, readers will learn the state of the art of 3D reconstruction and modeling' - Professor Takeo Kanade, Carnegie Mellon University.
