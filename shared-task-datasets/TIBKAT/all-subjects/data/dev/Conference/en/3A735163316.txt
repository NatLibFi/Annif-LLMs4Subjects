Context-aware systems and applications : first international conference, ICCASA 2012, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, November 26 - 27, 2012 ; revised selected papers
This book constitutes the thoroughly refereed proceedings of the first International Conference on Context-Aware Systems and Applications, ICCASA 2012, held in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, in November 2012. The papers cover a wide spectrum of issues in the area of Context-Aware Systems (CAS)
