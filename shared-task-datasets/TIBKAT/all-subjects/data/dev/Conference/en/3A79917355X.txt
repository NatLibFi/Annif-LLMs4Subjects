IEEE International Conference on Mechatronics and Automation (ICMA), 2014 : 3 - 6 Aug. 2014, Tianjin, China
Annotation, The objective of ICMA 2014 is to provide a forum for researchers, educators, engineers, and government officials involved in the general areas of mechatronics, robotics, automation and sensors to disseminate their latest research results and exchange views on the future research directions
