International Conference on Mechatronics and Control (ICMC), 2014 : 3 - 5 July 2014, Jinzhou, China ; conference proceedings
Annotation, The conference aims at creating a forum for scientists, engineers and practitioners throughout the world to present the latest technology advancement in mechatronics, systems and control The conference scope covers all theoretical and practical aspects of the related fields Specifically, the fields of interests include, but not limited to, modeling and design, signal, image and data Processing, intelligent control, advanced motion control, networked control, sensors and actuators and networks, fault detection and diagnosis, robotics, mobile platforms, unmanned vehicles, advanced manufacturing, automotive and ransportation systems, mechatronics in energy systems, human machine interface, mechatronics applications in cyberphysical systems, applications on industrial mechatronical System
